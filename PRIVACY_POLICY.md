# Privacy Policy for DeadTapes

**Effective Date:** March 17, 2026

## 1. Introduction
DeadTapes ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. Our fundamental principle is simple: **we do not collect, store, or share any of your personal data.**

This Privacy Policy applies to the DeadTapes mobile application (the "App") for iOS. 

## 2. Information We Do Not Collect
DeadTapes is designed to be a private, localized experience. 
*   **No Accounts:** You are never required to create an account or provide an email address.
*   **No Analytics:** We do not embed analytics trackers, telemetry, or crash reporting tools that report back to us.
*   **No Advertising:** We do not serve targeted ads or use advertising identifiers (IDFA).

## 3. Local Data Storage (SwiftData)
Any data created while using the App—such as your saved "Favorite" shows or playback history—is stored **locally on your personal device** using Apple's SwiftData framework. 
*   We do not have access to this data. 
*   This data never leaves your device and is not synchronized to any third-party servers controlled by us.

## 4. Third-Party Services
DeadTapes acts as a streaming client for publicly available audio. To provide this service, the App makes direct network requests to the **Internet Archive (archive.org)** API.

When you search for or stream a show, your device communicates directly with the Internet Archive. Your IP address and request data may be subject to the [Internet Archive's Privacy Policy](https://archive.org/about/terms.php). We do not act as an intermediary for these requests, nor do we intercept or log this traffic.

## 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time if the functionality of the App changes. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Effective Date."

## 6. Contact Us
If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via the support mechanisms provided in the repository's documentation or through the App Store listing.
